The Science
Physics

Full-Spectrum Absorption

Graphene is a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a hexagonal lattice. Its unique electronic band structure allows it to absorb photons across the entire electromagnetic spectrum — from ultraviolet through infrared.

No other known material does this.
